Sports

Football
Football is the most the popular sport all over the world. It was made up by English men who spread it all over the world afterwards. Football is played using a ball with 22 men divided into two teams on a huge field with 2 nets at each side. The game is 90 minutes long and the team that place the ball in the net more wins.
Handball
Handball is a sport that depends on the force of your hand and your accuracy while throwing a medium sized ball. It is played by 12 men divided into two teams on a flat court with 2 small nets at each side of the court. The game is divided into two halves of 30 minutes each. The team that places the ball in the net more wins.
Basketball
Basketball is a sport that depends on the height of a person and his accuracy in placing a large ball inside the net to go through it . It is most popular in the United States of America as they have the most popular league that is called NBA.
